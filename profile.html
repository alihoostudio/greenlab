About

Green lab is a multidisciplinary platform, based in Isfahan. Interested in collaborating and merging ideas in between art, technology & design. Our frame/work is to introduce new areas of thinking to local audience and make potential to work in between areas.

Co-Founder

Journalist, since 2007| Chief editor of “Baraye Farda”, a magazine based on Isfahan modern life and society 2013.2014 | Chief editor in "Shahr-e Emrooz”, an special issue magazine based on Isfahan Metropolitan in "Esfahan-e Emrooz" daily newspaper 2015-2017. In “share e emrooz” he tried to run a new style of urban journalism for Iranian newspapers that rise a better feeling of fixation to a city. The content was mainly the story of great unknown people, street atmosphere, and different aspects of urban life. This project persisted for 545 number issue. He is the Chief editor of “Kalan-Shahr” the isfahan’s first urban management Magazine, right now.
